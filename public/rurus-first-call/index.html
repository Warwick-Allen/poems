Ruru's First Call

Ruru's First Call
Warwick Allen
Sunday, 30 July 2017

Ruru wakens
Heart hastens
Is it really
No, can't be
That time again?
Again

Day's been
Done somethin'
Much not done
Racing the sun
The sun won
Again

Race fail
But my battle
—Important one
It is won
When I say “yes”
When I ask
“Did I love?”

(recording on Suno)
