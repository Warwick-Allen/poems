Armour
Warwick Allen
Sunday, 26 April 2026

[Verse 1]
O Lord, by Your enabling
Make us strong and unbending.
When our spirits are wrestling
Against slander and testing,
To the blood-stained cross we cling,
In Your righteousness trusting.

[Chorus 1]
Power in Your power,
Might in Your might,
In desperate hour,
Save us through the fight.

[Verse 2]
The rulers harass, harangue,
The devils conspire, plan,
But wicked schemes You defang.
E'er since Your blood freely ran,
Their threats empty, hollow rang.
We're safe in the Better Man.

[Chorus 2]
Safe in Your hand,
Under Your wing,
In desperate land,
Be our covering.

[Verse 3]
When spent all we entail,
We stand: You will avail.
When lies and doubts assail,
Those firey darts will fail.
You give wind in our sail,
You give faith to prevail.

[Chorus 3]
Safe in Your gaze,
Under Your eye,
In desperate days,
Attend to our cry.

[Verse 4]
May only truth our lips leave,
In each confession, each creed;
May only truth we believe.
And, o Lord, may each deed
We do be right, so to leave
Your peace and a gospel seed.

[Chorus 4]
Life in Your life,
It's You we breathe,
In desperate strife,
Meet us in our need.

[Verse 5]
We run to country farthest
When You create the passage.
We run up mountain highest
Carrying the good message.
Being the evangelist,
Freeing people from bondage.

[Chorus 5]
You know our straight,
Weak condition,
In desperate state,
We ask for action.
