My Thought Meandering

My Thought Meandering
Warwick Allen
Monday, 8 June 2026

I could share a line,
Or even two.
Something from my mind
I'd share with you.

Will you enter?
Do you dare?
Will you venture
To peek in there?

My thought meandering,
It would be seen.
You'd be wondering,
“What could it mean?”

Will you enter?
Do you dare?
Will you care
To see my fear?

(recording on Suno)
